Jolly Rancher Edible Straw

RECIPE

Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Grease a silicone mat and place four jolly rancher candies on top. Heat in oven for about 5 minutes until melted. Remove from heat and cool, allowing bubbles to release before wrapping candy around greased skewer. Roll candy into straw, using the back of a knife to release candy if required. Freeze until solid.

Drunken S’mores

RECIPE

Combine cake vodka, creme de cacao and heavy whipping cream in a shaker with ice. Shake well.Strain mix into a shot glass, leaving a little bit of room at the top. Use a spoon to layer on Bacardi 151. To serve, light the top layer and use it to roast marshmallows.

U-Boat

RECIPE

Place shot glass on upside down collins glass and fill with jagermeister. Place pint glass over the two glasses and flip everything. Remove collins glass and fill the pint glass with lager. As you drink, the jagermeister will leak into drink.
